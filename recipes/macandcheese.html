Mac and Cheese recipe

Description:

This cheesey mac and cheese is full of cheese and noodles.

Ingredients list

  • 1 box of Curly noodles
  • 1 block of cheddar cheese - shredded
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • 1 Tbs of flour
  • season to taste

Steps

  1. Cook noodles
  2. While noodles are cooking, melt butter in a pan
  3. Whisk in flour
  4. Once thickened, add milk slowly and keep whisking
  5. When combined and thickening, add in shredded cheese until melted
  6. Combine in cooked noodles and stir to mix
