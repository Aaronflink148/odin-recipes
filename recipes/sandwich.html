Sandwich Recipe

Description:

This recipe is to create the best sandwich you might ever have.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 slice of your favorite cheese
  • 3 ounces of sliced turkey
  • 1 slice of tomato
  • Diced onion
  • Sauce to spread

Steps:

  1. Assemble all ingredients
  2. Between the slices of bread, layer each of the other ingredients
  3. Spread the sauce on one or both insides of the bread.
